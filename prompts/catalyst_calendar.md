# Catalyst Calendar Prompt

Summarize upcoming catalysts that matter for candidates, holdings, and active theses.

## Catalyst types

- Earnings and guidance
- Investor days and product launches
- Regulatory or legal milestones
- Macro prints
- Filings and lockup expirations
- Thesis review deadlines

## Output

Group events by date, ticker, and decision relevance. Flag catalysts that require preparation before the event date.
