# Portfolio Review Prompt

Review current portfolio positions against active theses and risk limits.

## Focus

- Concentration by issuer, sector, factor, and catalyst date
- Thesis drift since entry
- Sizing versus conviction and downside
- Positions missing review dates
- Candidates that conflict with existing exposure

## Output

Return a table of actions: hold, trim, add, research, or exit. Include the reason and the evidence needed before acting.
