# Ticker Memo Prompt

Create a concise ticker memo for one security.

## Sections

1. Business snapshot
2. Why it is interesting now
3. Variant perception
4. Key numbers to verify
5. Catalysts
6. Risks and disconfirming evidence
7. Position fit
8. Next research action

## Rules

If source data is missing or stale, state that before giving an opinion. Do not invent financial metrics.
