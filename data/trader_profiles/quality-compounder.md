# Quality Compounder

## Philosophy

Own durable businesses that can reinvest capital at attractive incremental returns for a long time. The best ideas should become more obvious as evidence compounds.

## Looks for

- High and defensible returns on invested capital
- Recurring revenue or repeat purchase behavior
- Pricing power
- Conservative balance sheet
- Management with credible capital allocation

## Avoids

- Leverage-led equity stories
- One-product companies without renewal evidence
- Growth that requires permanent external capital
- Cheap stocks where quality is deteriorating

## Questions

- What protects margins five years from now?
- Is growth creating value or consuming capital?
- What evidence would prove this is not a compounder?
